Gnomish fruit batta

A delicious zesty & exotic fruit batta from far away lands

Using the Gnome famous Gianne Dough, this batta has a delectable chewy texture with deep and rich flavour coming from the secret mix of roasted gnomish flours.

  • Gianne dough
  • Orange chunks
  • Lime chunks
  • Pineapple chunks
  • Equa leaves
  1. Prepare Gianne dough in a batta tin as normal.
  2. Bake the dough for a short while.
  3. Top the batta with chunks of pineapple, orange and lime.
  4. Lay four sprigs of equa leaves on top of the batta before baking it in the oven once more.
  5. Finish the batta with a sprinkling of gnome spices.
